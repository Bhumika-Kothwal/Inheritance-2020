Inheritance is a summer mentorship program for first year students at VJTI to help them start their development journies.

Looking at Inheritance-2020 through numbers

Timeline

The Beginning

6 APRIL 2020

Forms for Inheritance 2020 released.

The Interviews

2 MAY 2020

Our members held an intensive interview session with each team to ensure they stay on track.

The Journey Commences

6 MAY 2020

Inheritance kicked off with mentors assigned to each team who would guide them throughout.

The Period of Hard Work

6 MAY 2020 - 5 JULY 2020

Over a period of 2 months, students worked hard to finish their websites, apps and data models.

The Submissions

6 JULY 2020

Students submitted their work and documentation to CoC and start getting ready for presentations.

The Presentations

11-12 JULY 2020

Time to show your projects in the Final Presentations.

The Conclusion

12 JULY 2020

Is it over though? Keep practising what you learnt and keep learning new things!
