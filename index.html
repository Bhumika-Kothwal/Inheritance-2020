Inheritance is a summer mentorship program for first year students at VJTI to help them start their development journies.

Total teams participated in Inheritance 2020 are 43 out of which 11 are Web Development, 16 are App Development, 7 are ML and Web Development, 7 are ML and App Development, 1 is Game Development and 1 is Blockchain. 34 mentors helped these teams for their project. Each team had minimum 2 mentors assigned and one mentor was assigned to at least 3 teams.
